# AutoStream Product Information

## Pricing Plans

### Basic Plan
- **Cost:** $29/month
- **Video Limit:** 10 videos/month
- **Resolution:** 720p resolution
- **Features:** Standard automated editing.

### Pro Plan
- **Cost:** $79/month
- **Video Limit:** Unlimited videos
- **Resolution:** 4K resolution
- **Features:** AI captions, Advanced transitions, Priority processing.

## Company Policies
- **Refunds:** No refunds after 7 days of purchase.
- **Support:** 24/7 support is available ONLY on the Pro plan.
- **Contact:** Support email is support@autostream.com.
